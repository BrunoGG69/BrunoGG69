## About
I'm interested in building intelligent systems that combine electronics, software, and artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems.

My interests span Electronics, Robotics, Embedded Systems, Edge AI, Computer Vision, and the Internet of Things (IoT). I enjoy turning ideas into working prototypes, from AI-powered applications to embedded systems and connected devices.

Before university, I served as Head Of Development of PARADIGM, my school's technology club, where I organized technical events, led student initiatives, and helped foster a stronger technology community.

I've worked on projects ranging from AI-powered attendance systems and computer vision applications to BLE-based indoor presence tracking and embedded IoT solutions. Through hackathons, competitions, and open-source projects, I've gained hands-on experience in both technical development and leadership.

My long-term goal is to work at the intersection of Electronics, Robotics, and Artificial Intelligence, building technologies that are intelligent, practical, and impactful.
